Introduction
  • Here's (a representation of) a box.
  • It exists in 3-dimensional space.
  • We can animate its movement by drawing it repeatedly in different positions.

  • By drawing it more frequently in more positions the movement is smoother and the animation nicer to watch.

    One way to ensure this is to keep a high framerate. We already do that here using requestAnimationFrame() so the other factor is to make sure the positions at which the box is rendered are continuous.
